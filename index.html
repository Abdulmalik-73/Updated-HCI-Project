Located in the prestigious Bole area of Addis Ababa, Ambassador Hotel offers world-class hospitality with Ethiopian warmth. Our hotel features luxurious rooms, exceptional dining, and modern amenities designed for both business and leisure travelers.

With over 20 years of excellence in hospitality, we pride ourselves on providing personalized service that exceeds expectations.
