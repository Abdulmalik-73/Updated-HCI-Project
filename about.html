About Ambassador Hotel

Excellence in Hospitality Since 2003

Our Story

Bole Ambassador Hotel has been a landmark of luxury and hospitality in Addis Ababa for over two decades. Located in the heart of Bole, Ethiopia's diplomatic and business district, we have welcomed guests from around the world with authentic Ethiopian warmth and world-class service.

Our commitment to excellence has made us the preferred choice for business travelers, diplomats, and tourists seeking an unforgettable experience in Ethiopia's vibrant capital.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional hospitality experiences that blend Ethiopian culture with international standards, creating memorable stays for every guest.

Our Vision

To be recognized as Ethiopia's premier luxury hotel, setting the standard for excellence in hospitality and service.

Our Values

Excellence, integrity, respect, and genuine Ethiopian hospitality guide everything we do.
